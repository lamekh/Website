Why Learn Customer Personality Types?

Some customers love your team’s manner, while others brush it off. Some customers want to pick a fight, while others won’t disclose what they really want. Sound familiar? Customers don’t share personalities; they vary from person to person. We’ll show you how to tailor your manner to each of the four main Customer Personality Types.

Each of our 6 video lessons covers a different aspect of Customer Personality Types. See below for the full topic list.

Introduction to Customer Personality Types (01:12)

Driver Personality Types (03:53)

Driver personality types are used to being in the front seat. If you’re not heading in the same direction they may skid right past you. Avoid the pitfalls and potholes with our tactics for dealing with driver personalities.
